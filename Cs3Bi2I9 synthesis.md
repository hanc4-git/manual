## **Cs<sub>3</sub>Bi<sub>2</sub>I<sub>9</sub> synthesis**
### solution process
1. Bi<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub> *9.3190 g* (0.02 mol) in HI (57 wt. % in H<sub>2</sub>O) 80 mL\
   *2Bi<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub> (s) + 8HI (aq) &rarr; 2(BiI<sub>4</sub>)<sup>-</sup> (aq) + HI (aq)

2. CsI *15.5886 g* (0.02 mol) in boiling H<sub>2</sub>O *20 mL*\
   *CsI (s) + H<sub>2</sub>O (l) &rarr; Cs<sup>+</sup> (aq) + I<sup>-</sup> (aq)*

3. stir\
   *2(BiI<sub>4</sub>)<sup>-</sup> (aq) + HI (aq) + 3Cs<sup>+</sup> (aq) + 3I<sup>-</sup> (aq) &rarr; Cs<sub>3</sub>Bi<sub>2</sub>I<sub>9</sub>&darr; (s) + HI (aq)*

4. keep stirring and cool to room temperature
5. **vacuum filtration** with 5:1 diluted HI *100***mL*\
   *Cs<sub>3</sub>Bi<sub>2</sub>I<sub>9</sub> (s) + HI (aq) &rarr; Cs<sub>3</sub>Bi<sub>2</sub>I<sub>9</sub> (s) + H<sup>+</sup> (aq) + I<sup>-</sup> (aq)*

6. **vacuum filtration** with *CH<sub>4</sub>O, methanol* *300***mL*\
   *Cs<sub>3</sub>Bi<sub>2</sub>I<sub>9</sub> (s) + H<sup>+</sup> (aq) + I<sup>-</sup> (aq) + CH<sub>4</sub>O &rarr; Cs<sub>3</sub>Bi<sub>2</sub>I<sub>9</sub> (s) + [CH<sub>3</sub>I](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iodomethane) (l) + H<sub>2</sub>O (l)*

7. vacuum drying Cs<sub>3</sub>Bi<sub>2</sub>I<sub>9</sub> *38***g* (0.297 mol)

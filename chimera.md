## transfer
1. `Your Modules\Chemical Inventory\Inventory Management\Update/Transfer Inventory`
2. select **Building** and **Room**
3. enter *barcode numbers* in **Scan/Type Barcode**

     - option to nonbarcode
         1. switch to **Statics**
         2. select **Building** and **Room**
